Sprout is an interactive education platform built for K–12 students and career readiness programs. It uses scenario-based simulations — covering financial literacy, AI, entrepreneurship, and real-world decision-making — to teach students how to think through complex situations, not just memorize facts. I've led full-stack product development, platform architecture, and AI learning integrations, and have worked directly with universities and school districts to co-design and pilot the curriculum.
