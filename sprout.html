Overview

What is
Sprout?

Sprout is a K–12 education platform built around the idea that students learn better by doing. Instead of passive instruction, Sprout puts students inside real-world scenarios — financial decisions, business tradeoffs, technology ethics, career choices — and asks them to reason through the outcome.

It's designed for both classroom use and career readiness programs, with curriculum co-designed alongside universities and school districts. The goal isn't engagement for its own sake — it's building the kind of thinking that actually transfers into adult life.
